$(a+b)^2=a^2+2ab+b^2$

$$ a^2-b^2=(a+b)(a-b) $$

\[ f(x)=e^x+2x+1 \]

\begin{equation}\label{eq1}
f(x)=e^x+2x+1
\end{equation}

Differentiating (\ref{eq1}), we get
\begin{equation}\label{eq2}
f'(x)=e^x+2
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}\label{eq3}
f''(x)=e^x
\end{equation}

On integration (\ref{eq3}), becomes
\[ \int f(x)\, dx =e^x +C \]

\[ \int_0^1 e^x\, dx = \left. e^x \right|_0^1 = e-1 \]
